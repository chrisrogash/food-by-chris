<500mlHot Water
1 cupRice

Instructions

  1. Dice Onion and brown over medium heat.
  2. Add 1 tsp chilli powder, 1 tsp cumin, 1tsp cinnamon, beef mince and brown.
  3. Add diced tomatoes, tomato puree, 1 tbsp oregano, and 1 beef stock cube (crumbled) - mix.
  4. Add a little bit of hot water, mix, bring to simmer (reduce heat slightly).
  5. Simmer with lid on for 20min. Stirring every ~5min.
  6. On the side, cook rice and leave to sit.
  7. Add drained and washed kidney beans to chilli mixture, stir, leave lid off for 10 minutes. Stirring every ~5min.
  8. Stir again, leave to rest for ~15min. Serve.

Notes

  • Makes 2 meals.
  • Also goes well with mashed/jacket potato.
  • Gluten Free (!) -> Ensure stock cube has no gluten.

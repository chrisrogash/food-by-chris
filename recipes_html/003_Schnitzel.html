Instructions

  1. Halve chicken fillets
  2. Prepare bowls for dipping chicken.
  3. Bowl 1: Combine plain flour and 2 tsp garlic powder.
  4. Bowl 2: 2 eggs, whisked.
  5. Bowl 3: Tandaco Stuffing Mix.
  6. Dip chicken fillets in each bowl, 1, then 2, then 3. Place to the side.
  7. fill fry pan (~1/4 to 1/2) with oil, place over medium heat.
  8. Cook coated chicken ~5-10 min each side until browned.
  9. Once cooked, place schnitzel on a rack to remove excess oil.
  10. Sprinkle schnitzels with salt flakes.

Notes

  • Buy ~1.5kg chicken from supermarket, but make sure it has 4 fillets. 2 (halved) for use now, can freeze remaining.
  • Makes 4 schnitzels

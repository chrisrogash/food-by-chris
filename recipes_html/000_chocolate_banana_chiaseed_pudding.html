2 tbspChia Seeds
1/2 cupAlmond or Oat Milk (any is fine)
1 1/2 tspMaple Syrup
1 tbspCocoa powder
1 tbspChocolate Chips
1 tbspCrushed Almonds
1Banana

Instructions

  1. Mix (stir) maple syrup, milk, cocoa powder, chia seeds together in a mason jar (place ingredients in that order).
  2. Wait ~5 minutes.
  3. Stir mixture again.
  4. Refridgerate minimum 4 hours.
  5. Slice banana, and place chocolate chips, crushed almonds, and banana on top.

Notes

  • Serves 1
  • 1x 300ml Mason jar per person.
  • Ideally refrigerate overnight.
  • Chocolate milk makes this richer.
  • Remove almonds for Nut Free.
  • Almond milk for Gluten Free.
  • Special choc chips required for Dairy Free.

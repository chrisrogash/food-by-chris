2 tbspChia Seeds

1Red Apple
2 tspCoconut Oil (or butter)

1 tspCinnamon
PinchCinnamon

Instructions

  1. Mix (stir) maple syrup, milk, apple sauce, chia seeds, and 1/4 tsp cinnamon together in a mason jar (place ingredients in that order).
  2. Wait ~5 minutes.
  3. Stir mixture again.
  4. Refridgerate minimum 4 hours.
  5. Peal and slice apple into edible chunks.
  6. Just before serving, cook apple chunks, 1 tbsp maple syrup, 1 tsp cinnamon in a saucepan lined with the coconut oil.
  7. Place toppings on top with a pinch of cinnamon.

Notes

  • Serves 1
  • 1x 300ml Mason jar per person.
  • Ideally refrigerate overnight.
  • Almond milk for Gluten Free.
  • Apple suggestions: Kanzi (EU), Envy (NZ), Pink Lady (WA), Bravo (WA), Cosmic Crisp (US) - in that order.
